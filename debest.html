Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

What are the school hours?
Our school hours are from 8:00 AM to 3:00 PM, Monday through Friday.
What is the student-teacher ratio?
We maintain a student-teacher ratio of approximately 20:1 to ensure personalized attention.
Are meals provided?
Yes, we provide nutritious meals prepared daily on campus, with options for special dietary needs.
How can I communicate with teachers?
You can communicate via our Students/Parents Platform or contact teachers during scheduled meetings.
