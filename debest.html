About Us

DEBEST ACADEMY is dedicated to nurturing growth and excellence in children from their early years (Creche) through Junior High School (JHS). And what we do is to provide holistic education, balancing academics with character building, arts, and sports, ensuring each student’s full potential is realized.

MISSION

We advance knowledge by creating a conducive environment to apply knowledge that equips students with confidence leadership through quality teaching, care and morally based training that opens up excellent academic future.

VISION

To be recognised as an outstanding top private institution offering the highest quality of education with strong religious, moral and academic training that shapes the students for life.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

What are the school hours?
Our school hours are from 8:00 AM to 3:00 PM, Monday through Friday.
What is the student-teacher ratio?
We maintain a student-teacher ratio of about approximately 20:1 to ensure personal attention.
Are meals provided?
YES, we provide nutrituous meals prepared daily, with options for special dietary needs.
How can I communicate with teachers?
You can communicate via our student/parent platform or contact teachers during scheduled meetings.
