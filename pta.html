Parent/Teacher Association (PTA)

Welcome to the Parent/Teacher Association

The PTA at DEBEST ACADEMY is a collaborative group of parents and teachers dedicated to enhancing the educational experience for our students. We work together to support school activities, organize events, and foster a strong school community.

Our Mission

To promote open communication between parents and teachers, support student learning, and contribute to the overall well-being of the school community.

Get Involved

We encourage all parents and teachers to join the PTA. Your involvement can make a significant difference in our school. Attend meetings, volunteer for events, and share your ideas!

Upcoming Events

  • School Fundraiser - March 15th
  • Family Fun Day - April 20th
